Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a traditional italian dish prepared by making the pasta al dente, frying some chopped up bacon under light fire, turning off the heat, mixing the strained pasta with the bacon and eggs, and finishing with lots of parmesan or pecorino cheese and black pepper

  • Spaghetti - 200 grams
  • Bacon - 50 grams
  • Eggs - 2 full eggs and 2 yolks
  • Parmesan/pecorino cheese - 50 grams
  • Black pepper
  1. Boil the pasta for 5 minutes or until it is al dente
  2. Slowly fry the bacon until golden
  3. Turn off the head of the pan where the bacon was cooked
  4. Mix the pasta, the bacon, and the eggs. Beat gently
  5. Serve on a plate
  6. Decorate with the grated cheese and add the black pepper

Ham & Cheese Pie

Description

Ham and cheese family recipe. Delicious and hearty dish that feeds 3 people.

  • Ham - 400 grams
  • Cheese - 400 grams
  • Hardbolied eggs - 2
  • Pie dough - 2
  • Mustard
  1. Pre-heat the oven at 190-200 degrees Celcius
  2. Hard boil 2 eggs (10 minutes after the first bubbles appear)
  3. Spread mustard on a face of each pie dough
  4. Place half the amount of ham on one of the pie doughs
  5. On top of them, place half the total amount of cheese
  6. Chop the boiled eggs and place on top of the cheese
  7. Place the rest of the cheese on top of the chopped, hard boiled eggs
  8. Place the rest of the ham on top of the cheese
  9. Place the other pie dough on top of the ham (the face with the mustard face down)
  10. Close the doughs together
  11. Paint the top of the pie with more mustard
  12. Stab it a few times with a knife to allow hot air to exit the pie
  13. Cook for 10-15 minutes on the bottom and 5-10 on the top

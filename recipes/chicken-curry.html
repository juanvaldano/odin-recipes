Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken curry with a slight spicy-sweet finish, guaranteed to fill up 4 guests

  • Curry mix - 15 grams
  • Chicken - 2 breasts
  • Long grain rice - 60-80 grams per guest
  • Garlic - 4 cloves
  • Onion - 2
  • Eggaplant - 1
  • Carrot - 2
  • Peanut - 25 grams
  • Cream - 200 ml
  • Coconut milk - 20 ml
  • Butter - 50 grams
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Peperoncino
  1. Get enough water boiling to cover the full amount of the rice
  2. Boil the rice for 12 minutes, then strain it. Reserve
  3. Chop the carrots and eggplant in 2 cm. by 2 cm. cubes
  4. Mince the garlic
  5. Cut the onions into thin slices
  6. Chop the chicken breasts into cubes
  7. Sautee the chicken cubes until golden. Reserve
  8. Mix all the raw, cut vegetables in the same pot and cook them until slightly soft
  9. Add the cooked chicken cubes into the mix
  10. Add the cream, butter, and curry mix
  11. Cook for 5 minutes on slow fire
  12. Turn off the heat and add the salt, pepper, and peperoncino
  13. Roast the peanuts over slow fire
  14. Chop the peanuts into chunks
  15. Serve half a plate of chicken curry, half with rice
  16. Decorate with the chopped peanuts
